Who are we?

We are a bunch of enthusiastic sophomore students studying engineering at NITK Surathkal looking to transform the current market for cycle transportation.

What do we do?

Right now we just provide cycles on a rental basis for the one or more semester/s . Additionally, this is provided with complimentary repair services for the same period.

Why us?

Right now we provide on-demand services , with an excellent customer service record . Our priority is to ensure satisfaction amongst our customers!

Future Plans

We plan to introduce a pick-and-drop service from next semester onwards along with the release of our Android App to book cycles on a minute-basis.
