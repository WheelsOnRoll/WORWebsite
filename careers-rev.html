Why should you join Wheels On Roll?

"What you think, you create. What you feel, you attract. What you imagine, you become."
We are looking for a dedicated and passionate individual who is a good team-player. We believe in our philosophy, that an organisation is made by people not by the money in it.
Change the World

We work to make the world a better place . However it's not possible to change alone and we are looking for torchbearers.
Join the fight agaist Climate Change

Our world is facing a true planetary emergency. Climate change has a profound impact on survival and development of mankind. The world needs WORroirs and we at WOR make sure we leave a better planet than the one that we came in!
Work freely

We believe that an informal network is more efficient as people tend to work in collaborative ways. We at WOR have a very informal work culture with no fixed timings and an informal dress code at work.
Learn and Teach

“Learn to light a candle in the darkest moments of someone’s life. Be the light that helps others see; it is what gives life its deepest significance.” ― Roy T. Bennett.
We make sure that you learn more than what you know and give back that knowledge to someone who doesn't have it !
